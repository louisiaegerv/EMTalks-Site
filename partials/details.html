The details

The event

EMtalks is a place where creativity and individuality is celebrated. The day-long event invites you to listen to thought-provoking, inspiring, and personal stories that come from our people all over the company. You can think of it as a place where our most passionate people gather to listen to, share with, and challenge each other to make the world just a little bit better.

The theme

Sometimes, the corporate world seems like an endless sea of titles, they may explain what you do from 8 to 5, but you are so much more. Untitled shines a light on all the hats you wear in and out of office. What do you do when you’re done with your work day 1 template ? Are you a creative writer? Are you a firefighter? Perhaps you are a crazy cat lady? In the world of untitled, you are entitled to as many hats as you want. And they all get the spotlight.

Nominate

Anyone that works for ExxonMobil can be nominated, regardless of years of service or position. The only requirements are inspiring personality, a story to tell and eagerness to take it to the EMtalks stage. Speakers are selected through a companywide nomination process.
